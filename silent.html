SILENT SQUASH

Watch with no distractions!

A unique style of squash coaching videos.

INTRODUCTION

Watch the video or read the text, it's your choice.

Videos are the perfect combination of sound and vision. But sometimes the sound and voices can be distracting.

All the videos in the Silent Squash series have no sound at all. They focus on images and text. They can be paused at any time and the viewer can take their time to read the explanation.

Not all my videos will be released as Silent Squash versions, just the coaching, skill and technique ones. They will almost always be shorted than the sound versions too.

I plan to release the Silent Squash version a few days after the sound version. I also plan to create Silent Squash versions of some of my older videos.

In addition to the video, I also create a page with images and text for those that prefer to read and look at the photos. The video and page are both linked to in the table below.
