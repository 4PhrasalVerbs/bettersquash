ABOUT

Nothing is better than having a lesson with a coach, especially when you first start to play squash, but not everybody has that option.

I created these videos to help new players and complete beginners understand the basics of squash, without making any assumptions of their previous knowledge or experience.

Each video is quite short, most around 3 minutes, some are a little bit more. The videos can be watched in any order, although there is a natural progression through the series. Only watch the videos that interest you.

This website is static, which means it doesn't have a comment section like some websites. If you have any comments, questions, feedback, complaints or compliments, please leave them on the video's page itself. Alternatively, you can email me directly.
