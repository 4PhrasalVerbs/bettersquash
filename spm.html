GOING SOLO

OVERVIEW
You are not going to reach your potential as a squash player unless you spend a certain amount of time on court alone. Some people think of this time as solitary confinement - a punishment for players who can't keep the ball tight when they need to. Others, and I hope you count yourself in this group, see it as an opportunity to strengthen their weaknesses and forge their signature shot.
